Jokers Bank


Jokers Bank is a token distribution application


Stake CLOWN and MEME in Jokers Bank*

You can stake any amount of CLOWN and max 100k MEME tokens at any time in the Jokers Bank.

Staking rewards distribution:
- 14% of the CLOWN amount staked after 7 days - reward is JOKERS,
- 7% of MEME amount staked after 7 days - reward is LOL

For the first time you can claim reward immediately after placing your stake (no need to wait 7 days).
You have to wait 7 days to claim reward again.
You can claim staking reward later (e.g. in a month), but you will receive 14% (when staking CLOWN) or 7% (when staking MEME) of your balance staked, but
if you claim every week you can get up to 730% or 365% per year.

You can unstake at any time.

You need MintMe for blockchain transaction fee.
A small amount of MintMe is included in the transaction fee at the time the LOL claim is executed. It is used to repurchase and increase liquidity.

You must have MetaMask wallet (or other wallet) configured for MintMe.
Reload the page if Jokers Bank is not connected to MetaMask or "undefined".
